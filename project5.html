Selected Work

La Lumière Luxury Goods

La Lumiere is a Greece-based luxury brand launched in early 2025, offering exquisitely crafted luxury candles and high-end goods designed to elevate everyday experiences. The project focused on establishing a cohesive visual and experiential identity that reflects sophistication, elegance, and timeless aesthetics across both physical and digital touchpoints.
As the lead creative for La Lumiere, I directed the brand from concept to market. This included designing the logo and product packaging, shaping the overall creative direction, and developing social media content and marketing strategy. Every detail was carefully crafted to convey a sense of exclusivity and refinement, ensuring the brand resonated with discerning customers.

Branding Innovation Strategy

Brand Identity & Visual Language

Bringing a luxury brand to life required navigating both creative challenges and strategic opportunities. Each design iteration—from logo refinement to product packaging and marketing visuals—allowed me to strengthen my skills in high-end branding and storytelling. La Lumiere became a defining project that reinforced my ability to translate abstract luxury concepts into tangible, captivating designs.
The visual language of La Lumiere emphasizes understated elegance and minimalist luxury. From the modern, refined logo to meticulously designed packaging, each element reflects the brand’s commitment to sophistication and craftsmanship. My creative direction ensured that every touchpoint—from products to digital presence—communicates a cohesive, aspirational identity that positions La Lumiere at the pinnacle of luxury goods.

Digital Presence & Marketing Strategy

I led the creation of La Lumiere’s digital and social presence, translating the brand’s luxury ethos into a polished online experience. Social media content, visual storytelling, and marketing campaigns were designed to communicate exclusivity, elevate the brand narrative, and engage a sophisticated audience.
The approach focused on seamless visual consistency and intuitive digital experiences, ensuring that each interaction—whether on social media, the website, or marketing channels—reflects the refined aesthetic and aspirational lifestyle of La Lumiere. This phase highlights my expertise in marrying creative direction with strategic brand communication.
