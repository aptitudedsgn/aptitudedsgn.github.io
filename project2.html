Selected Work

Herodotus Travel

My work with Herodotus Travel began in September 2022, where I joined the company as a dedicated Graphic Designer. The objective was to elevate and unify the brand’s visual presence across all touchpoints, ensuring a modern, consistent, and engaging identity that could support both marketing growth and customer trust. My contributions spanned digital, social, and print mediums, focusing on strengthening brand recognition, improving visual cohesion, and creating compelling design assets that clearly communicated the company’s travel offerings and destinations.
As the in-house Graphic Designer, I was responsible for managing and evolving Herodotus Travel’s entire visual identity. I worked across multiple platforms and formats, designing assets that aligned with the brand’s values while supporting ongoing marketing initiatives.
My responsibilities included creating social media content such as posts, stories, and reels, executing full social media campaigns, and designing advertisements, posters, banners, and promotional videos. I also developed newsletters, digital storefront promos, catalogues, and printed magazines, ensuring consistency and clarity across all customer-facing materials.

Branding Innovation Strategy

Creative Process & Growth

Working in a fast-paced travel environment required adaptability and a strong sense of visual strategy. Each campaign and deliverable involved balancing creativity with clarity, ensuring designs were both visually engaging and informative. Through continuous iteration and collaboration with the marketing team, I refined my ability to translate business goals into cohesive visual solutions. This role significantly strengthened my skills in large-scale brand management and cross-platform design execution.

A central focus of my work was maintaining and evolving a cohesive visual language across all platforms. This included typography, color systems, layout consistency, and visual hierarchy, ensuring that every touchpoint felt unmistakably Herodotus Travel.
A key milestone was the redesign of the company logo, aimed at modernizing the brand and improving recognition. The updated identity provided a stronger foundation for future campaigns and reinforced a more contemporary, professional image aligned with the company’s growth.

Marketing, Content & Customer Experience

Beyond brand identity, I played a major role in shaping the customer experience through design. From eye-catching digital storefront promos to informative newsletters and immersive catalogues, each piece was designed to guide, inspire, and inform potential travelers.
Printed magazines and catalogues were carefully structured to communicate destinations and services clearly, while promotional videos and social content brought experiences to life. Together, these efforts enhanced engagement, improved brand perception, and strengthened the overall marketing strategy.
